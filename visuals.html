Isometric 3D Graphics

While working at Binfinity, I was asked to create some graphics to use on our site and banner advertisements. I had the idea of making isometric representations of the blockchain and cryptocurrencies. I wanted to make them look like a circuit board with the signal traces or buildings connected by streets (think Tron). I decided to try and learn some 3D modeling to generate these images. While they are fairly simple, I learned a lot about 3D modeling and rendering.

Letterpress Works

Here are a few of the pieces I helped create while working at Bell'Invito Stationers. I created the designs, exported them for die creation, which were then printed. The processes used include letterpress printing, gold embossing, foil edging, envelope die cutting and Japanese silkscreen envelope liner die cutting.
